E-commerce solutions

Payments for both of our sites are processed using a Braintree custom integration. Clients can add payment methods, purchase packages for coaches, and monitor their order status. Providers receive notifications about new bookings and can use credits from the purchase to schedule lessons. Our system also monitors and tracks providers receiving payment for lessons.

Recurring billing and scheduling

In fall 2015, we made the biggest change to our scheduling platform, allowing for providers to setup recurring payments and charge clients on a per-lesson basis. This involved a significant re-engineering of our upfront order-based system, which was inspired by the APIs of services like Chargify and Recurrly.

Salesforce integrations

Salesforce is one of the most powerful pieces of the MyTennisLessons/FitnessTrainer platform, and is a critical piece for any medium-large sized business. Our Salesforce integration lets our team stay on top of potential clients, track instructor applications, and provides a data-hub for a lot of our marketing integrations. In 2015 we integrated with Heroku Connect, making our Salesforce integration incredibly easy to manage and allowing our Salesforce data to become a powerful tool for new data-driven applications.

Custom SEO engine

In 2013 we switched to a rails driven seo page generator. Important things to consider when building an SEO generator are defaults and overrides for content and seo tags, CDN caching for maximum performance, and ease-of-use by end users. Creating new pages and changing content shouldn't take developer time. Today our SEO engine generates tens of thousands of pages for both MyTennisLessons and FitnessTrainer, allowing us to easily expand our nationwide network and launch new markets, with no additional development required.

Dynamic geo-search results

One of the core features of both MyTennisLessons and FitnessTrainer is the ability to find a coach near you. We use Algolia to index all of our service-providers and deliver an quick and responsive search experience. The initial implementation was set in place by our Lead Developer from 2013-2015, Luke Wendling, but since then I've used the framework we built to power email campaigns, custom recommendation pages, and admin-search interfaces. Most recently I implemented a custom node script that can be used in Zapier to find the closest 3 providers, which can then be used in marketing emails.

Lead generation and drip campaigns

One of the biggest challenges we've faced is building dynamic drip campaigns that factor in the user's actions on the website, sales person's interactions with the user, and the lifecycle stage of users in our signup process, either as a provider or client.

Email marketing solutions

Over the course of five years, email has played an important role in growing our sports network. I've implemented solutions ranging from custom Rails drip mailers, Customer.io, Mailchimp, Hubspot, and Act-On to try and find the perfect balance of ease-of-access by marketing end-users and effective segment targeting. Today I'm happy to say we've found our best balance yet by using SendWithUs for amazingly easy to access templates that work for in-app emails, drip campaigns, and marketing blasts. This lets us change, test, and grow our email templates without costly code changes.

Admin user interfaces

I've spoken with many people over the years looking to setup or running a marketplace, and almost always I have to remind people about one of the most important users of the system..Admins. A lot of focus when building a system goes into the service provider and recipient user's experiences. But invariably any marketplace service is going to have to play some sort of role in-between these two sides of the service. Making sure that admin users can easily access important user data, see reports to understand the health of the business, and provide quick and efficient support means that you can provide a best-in-class service.

Most of our admin interfaces are custom built using ActiveAdmin, a delightful gem that makes simple, but powerful admin interfaces a breeze to build. More and more though I've been pushing functionality into saleforce using custom webhooks and Zapier.
